  • 9 November, 2024

  1. Mark the Starting and Ending Points

    2. Begin by marking the initial and final points for your sketch, which will guide your drawing.

  2. Use Light Lines

    3. Start by drawing with very light lines to establish the shape and proportions.

  3. Work from Start to End

    4. Draw your sketch by following the path from the starting point to the ending point.
